Who Am I ?

I am a recent graduate from Kakatiya University, India, with a degree in B.Com (Computers). Throughout my academic journey, I developed a passion for front-end development, where I find joy in crafting immersive user experiences through code. My ambition is to blend my knowledge of computer science with my expertise in front-end technologies, particularly React and Tailwind CSS, to create visually stunning and highly functional web applications. With a strong foundation in computer science concepts and my proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS, I am equipped to tackle complex challenges in web development. I am deeply fascinated by the ever-evolving landscape of web technologies and am committed to continuous learning and growth in this dynamic field.
